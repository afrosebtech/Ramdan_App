💰 Fitra & Zakat
🌙
Sadqa-e-Fitra
Eid ul Fitr se pehle ada karna farz hai. Har Muslim mard, aurat aur bachche ki taraf se dena zaroori hai jo nisaab ka maalik ho.
💵 Amount: Approx ₹100–₹150 per person
⏰ Waqt: Eid ki namaz se pehle
📌 Gehun, jau, khajoor, kishmish ya unki qeemat se ada kar sakte hain
💎
Zakat
Jo shakhs nisaab ka maalik ho — yaani 7.5 tola sona (87.48g) ya 52.5 tola chandi (612.36g) ya uske barabar maal — use saal mein ek baar Zakat dena farz hai.
💵 Amount: Kul maal ka 2.5%
⏰ Waqt: Saal pura hone par (Ramadan best waqt hai)
📌 Zakat se dil paak hota hai aur maal mein barkat aati hai 🤲
